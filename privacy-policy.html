6ix Messenger Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Thank you for choosing 6ix Messenger (“we,” “us,” or “our”), a 6Clement Joshua Group service. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy rights. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: Phone number, display name, profile picture, verification status, and account settings.
  • Contacts and Social Graph: With your consent, we access your contacts to help you connect with people you know who also use 6ix Messenger.
  • Communications: All messages, calls, and media are end-to-end encrypted. We do not store the content of your messages on our servers after delivery.
  • Verification Data: Identity documents, photos, videos, and other materials you provide to obtain verified or star account status.
  • Coins and Wallet: Transaction records, wallet balances, promotions, and gifting activities.
  • Usage and Device Information: Logs related to app performance, error reports, diagnostic information, and general usage statistics.
  • Location Data: With your consent, approximate or precise location information to enable location-based services or fraud prevention.
  • Promotions and Engagement: Records of your participation in reward programs, promotions, and monetization features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our services.
  • Verify your identity and manage verification badges.
  • Enable coins gifting, wallet transfers, and earnings management.
  • Prevent fraud, spam, and abuse.
  • Improve app performance and troubleshoot issues.
  • Personalize your experience and deliver relevant content.
  • Conduct analytics, research, and development.
  • Comply with legal obligations under applicable laws and regulations.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may disclose your data:

  • To our affiliates and subsidiaries under common ownership or control.
  • To service providers who perform functions on our behalf (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics).
  • To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government regulations.
  • To investigate, prevent, or take action against potential violations of our Terms of Service.
  • In connection with a merger, sale, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business.

4. Coins, Wallet, and Monetization

6ix Messenger offers digital coins and wallet balances (“6ix Coins”) which may be earned, gifted, or purchased. Your coin balances are associated with your account and are subject to our Terms of Service. Coins may be forfeited if you engage in prohibited conduct, including fraud, manipulation, or abuse of promotions.

We may collect and store transaction information, including the sender, recipient, date, amount, and method of transfer. Wallet balances are not insured and have no cash value outside of the platform unless expressly stated.

5. Verification and Badges

We provide different verification tiers, including Blue Ticks, Star Ticks, White Ticks, and Green Ticks. To verify your account, we may request identification documents, video verification, or other evidence of authenticity. Verified status may be revoked at any time if we determine the information provided was false or misleading.

Users are prohibited from impersonating individuals, government entities, or organizations. Verified badges do not imply endorsement or partnership.

6. AI Features and Voice Processing

6ix Messenger includes optional AI-powered features, such as voice commands and AI assistance. Voice inputs may be temporarily processed to enable the requested actions. These features are designed to respect your privacy and comply with data protection laws. We do not store voice recordings unless you explicitly consent.

7. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract (providing services you request).
  • Compliance with legal obligations.
  • Legitimate interests (improving and securing our services).
  • Your consent (for optional features and marketing).

8. Marketing, Promotions, and Communications

We may use your contact information to send you service-related notices, updates, and marketing materials. You can opt out of marketing messages at any time by using the unsubscribe link in the communication or adjusting your preferences in the app settings.

Promotional campaigns and referral programs are subject to specific terms, which may include eligibility criteria and limitations. Abuse or fraudulent participation in promotions may result in disqualification, forfeiture of rewards, or suspension of your account.

9. Analytics and Performance Data

We use internal and third-party analytics tools to understand how users interact with our services, measure performance, and improve features. This may include aggregated metrics such as page views, clicks, crash logs, and device characteristics. These analytics do not include the content of your messages.

We may also collect limited information from cookies and similar technologies to help maintain your session and personalize your experience.

10. Data Retention and Account Deletion

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by applicable law. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your data within a reasonable period (typically 30 to 90 days), subject to exceptions:

  • Data necessary to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes may be retained longer.
  • Transaction records related to coins and wallet balances may be retained for audit and compliance purposes.
  • Backups and cached copies may persist temporarily but will be purged in the ordinary course of business.

11. Security Measures

We implement reasonable physical, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your data. Examples include encryption in transit, secure server environments, and access controls. However, no system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

We encourage you to use a strong password, enable additional security features if available, and promptly report any suspicious activity to our support team.

12. Children’s Privacy

6ix Messenger is not intended for children under 13, or under the minimum age required in your jurisdiction. If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child without parental consent, we will promptly delete it. Parents or guardians who believe that their child has provided personal data should contact us immediately.

13. Automated Decision-Making and Profiling

Certain features, such as verification recommendations, fraud detection, or personalized suggestions, may involve limited automated processing. These processes are designed to improve your experience and safeguard our services. You can request human review or object to certain decisions by contacting our support team.
